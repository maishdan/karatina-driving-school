Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for driving school financing?

You can apply via our Apply Financing page. We support local and international financing programs.

Which payment methods are supported?

We support M-Pesa, card payments (Visa/Mastercard), PayPal, Worldcoin, and Bitcoin.

Do you offer weekend classes?

Yes! We offer evening and weekend driving classes for full-time workers and students.

Can I register from outside Kenya?

Yes! We welcome international students and provide remote consultation and e-learning options.
