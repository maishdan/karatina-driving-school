About Us

Karatina Driving School is one of Kenya’s most recognized and internationally-aligned driving academies. Based in Nyeri County and serving students globally, we’re known for delivering excellence in motor vehicle training and road safety education.

With over 10 years of experience, our NTSA-certified instructors train students across multiple categories: motorcycles (A), private cars (B), and commercial vehicles (C, CE).

  • ✅ Global Student Support – international registration, visa assistance
  • ✅ Multilingual Trainers – Swahili, English, French, German
  • ✅ Virtual Driving Simulators – remote training options
  • ✅ Online Certificates – downloadable, verifiable globally

We are committed to empowering responsible, confident drivers. Whether you're from Kenya, Nigeria, USA, or the UK — Karatina Driving School is your road to success.
